The Chicago Cubs

History:

The Cubs started in 1876 as the Chicago White Stockings and were also named the Colts (1890-1897) and Orphans (1898-1902) before becoming the Cubs in 1903. They also had an alleged curse hanging over their heads for more than 70 years. The start of the curse came during the 1945 World Series when, during Game 4, Billy Goat Tavern owner William Sianis put a curse on the Cubs after he and his pet goat were forced to leave Wrigley Field.
