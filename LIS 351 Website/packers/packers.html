The Green Bay Packers

History:

The Green Bay Packers are one of the most storied franchises in the NFL. Founded in 1919, the Packers are the oldest team in the league, and have a rich history of championships and success. Since their inception, the Green Bay Packers have won 13 league championships, including four Super Bowls. From their humble beginnings to their current status as one of the most beloved teams in the NFL, the Green Bay Packers have earned their place as one of the greatest sports franchises of all time.
